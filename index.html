Types of Dumpster Rentals Available


    Here are some common types of dumpster rentals you might find in Ocala:
  • Residential Dumpster: These are smaller dumpsters designed for residential use, typically ranging from 10 to 20 cubic yards. They suit home cleanouts, small renovation projects, or yard waste removal.
  • Construction Dumpster: Construction dumpsters are larger and more robust, often ranging from 20 to 40 cubic yards. They are used for construction and demolition debris disposal, such as debris from remodeling, roofing, or large-scale construction projects.
  • Roll-Off Dumpster: Roll-off dumpsters are open-top containers delivered to your location by trucks equipped with rollers. They come in various sizes, usually 10 to 40 cubic yards. They are ideal for large projects and can quickly load heavy debris using a walk-in door.
  • Commercial Dumpster: These are larger dumpsters designed for businesses and commercial establishments. They can handle significant waste and are available in various sizes to meet different commercial needs.
  • Compactor Dumpster: Compactor dumpsters are designed to compress waste, allowing for more efficient use of space and reducing pickups’ frequency. They are commonly used in commercial settings like restaurants, retail stores, and apartment complexes.
  • Concrete or Asphalt Dumpster: Specialized dumpsters are available to handle heavy and dense materials for projects involving concrete or asphalt removal.
  • Organic Waste Dumpster: If you want to dispose of green waste, like yard clippings, branches, and other organic materials, you may find specific dumpsters designed for organic waste disposal.
